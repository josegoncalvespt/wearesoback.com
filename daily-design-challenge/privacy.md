This Privacy Policy describes how the **Daily Design Challenge** iOS app (the "App") handles your information. The App is published by **So Back** ("we", "us"). Please read this policy carefully.

## TL;DR

- We do **not** collect, sell, or share your personal data.
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing SDKs.
- Your onboarding answers and progress live **only on your device**. We never see them.
- Subscription billing is handled by **Apple** and **RevenueCat**. We see anonymous subscription status only.

## What information the App handles

### Stored on your device only

- **Onboarding preferences** (your design focus, experience level, etc.) — stored in `UserDefaults`.
- **Challenge progress and streaks** — stored locally via `SwiftData`.

These never leave your device. Uninstalling the App deletes them.

### Sent to our backend (Supabase)

The App fetches published content (challenges, videos, inspiration, profiles) from our Supabase database over HTTPS. These are **read-only** requests for public content. No user-identifying data is sent.

### Sent to Apple and RevenueCat

When you subscribe or restore a purchase:

- **Apple's App Store** processes the payment (see [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)).
- **RevenueCat** receives an anonymous subscription identifier so the App can confirm your subscription status. RevenueCat is a subscription-management service. See [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

We do **not** receive your name, email, payment details, or Apple ID.

### Third-party content shown in the App

The App displays images and links from:

- **YouTube** (thumbnails via `img.youtube.com`) — see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Apple iTunes Search API** (app artwork via `itunes.apple.com`) — see Apple's policy above.
- **unavatar.io** (public profile avatars) — see [unavatar.io](https://unavatar.io).

These services receive a standard HTTPS request from your device when their content loads. No identifying information is attached.

## What we do not do

- We do **not** require a user account.
- We do **not** collect location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data.
- We do **not** use cookies, advertising identifiers (IDFA), or attribution SDKs.
- We do **not** sell or share data with third parties for marketing.
- We do **not** profile you for advertising.

## Children's privacy

The App is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content, but it is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Your rights

Because we hold no personal data tied to you, there is nothing to export or delete on our side. To remove the App's local data, delete the App from your device.

To manage or cancel your subscription, use the App Store directly:
**Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**, or visit [Apple's subscription help](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039).

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new policy at this URL. Continued use of the App after a change means you accept the updated policy.
