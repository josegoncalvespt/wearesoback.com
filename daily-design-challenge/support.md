## Frequently asked questions

### How do I restore my subscription?

If you reinstalled the App or signed in on a new device, tap **Restore Purchases** on the paywall screen. Your subscription will reactivate automatically once Apple confirms it.

### How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID, not through us. To cancel or change your plan:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **name** at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Tap **Daily Design Challenge**, then **Cancel Subscription**.

Detailed instructions: [Apple's subscription help](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039).

Cancelling stops auto-renewal. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

### Will I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Request one at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

### Where is my progress / streak stored?

Locally on your device. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes your progress. We do not have a backup.

### Does the App work offline?

The App ships with the full challenge library bundled, so it works offline once loaded. Video tutorials and avatar images require an internet connection.

### Which iOS versions are supported?

iOS 18.0 and newer.

### Found a bug? Have a feature request?

Email **wearesobackltd@gmail.com** with:

- Your iPhone model and iOS version.
- A short description of what happened.
- A screenshot or screen recording if possible.
