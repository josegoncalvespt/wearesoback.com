## Frequently asked questions

### How do I restore my subscription?

If you reinstalled the App or signed in on a new device, tap **Restore Purchases** in Settings. Your subscription will reactivate once Apple confirms it.

### How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID, not through us. To cancel or change your plan:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **name** at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Tap **How to Wash**, then **Cancel Subscription**.

Detailed instructions: [Apple's subscription help](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039).

Cancelling stops auto-renewal. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

### Will I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Request one at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

### Where is my scan history stored?

Locally on your device. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes every photo, note, and history entry. We do not have a backup.

### Does the App work offline?

Yes. Care-label decoding, the symbol library, and stain advice all run on-device. An internet connection is only required the first time you subscribe or when you restore a purchase.

### The OCR did not pick up the text on my label. What can I do?

Try a closer shot in better light, hold the phone parallel to the label, and make sure the label is flat. If a label has text on both sides, the App lets you capture a second photo for the reverse side.

### A care symbol I expected is missing from the picker.

The App covers the 40 most common ISO 3758 symbols. If a symbol you need is missing, email us with a photo of the label and we will add it in a future update.

### Which iOS versions are supported?

iOS 26.0 and newer.

### Found a bug? Have a feature request?

Email **wearesobackltd@gmail.com** with:

- Your iPhone model and iOS version.
- A short description of what happened.
- A screenshot or screen recording if possible.

If you have diagnostic reports in Settings → Diagnostic Reports, attaching one helps us reproduce the issue.
