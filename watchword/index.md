---
title: Watchword
description: A shared family code word that stops voice-clone scam calls. iPhone app.
---

A native iPhone app that gives your family a shared, automatically rotating code word. If a caller claiming to be family can't say today's word, hang up. Words are derived on-device from a secret only your family shares.
