## Frequently asked questions

### What is Watchword, in one sentence?

Your family shares a secret code word that changes automatically — if a caller claiming to be family can't say today's word, hang up and call them back on a number you know.

### My family member's phone shows a different word. Why?

Both phones derive the word from the same shared secret, so they should always match. If they don't:

1. Make sure both phones have the App open (the word refreshes when the App opens).
2. Check both phones are in the **same family** (Family tab → same family name and members).
3. If someone tapped **Rotate now**, other phones pick up the new word the next time they open the App.
4. Check both phones are signed into iCloud and online, then reopen the App.

### How do I invite a family member?

Family tab → **Add a family member** → send the invitation link by Messages or email. They tap the link on their iPhone, and iOS walks them through joining. They'll need the App installed.

### My parent/grandparent got the invite but nothing happened.

The link must be opened **on the iPhone that has Watchword installed**, signed into their own iCloud account. Have them open Messages or Mail on that phone and tap the blue link. If it still fails, delete and resend the invitation.

### Does the App work offline?

Yes, after setup. Words are computed on-device, so today's word shows even with no connection. You only need internet to join a family, sync a manual rotation, or manage your subscription.

### Does Watchword see our code words?

No. Words are never transmitted or stored anywhere — each phone computes them locally from a shared secret that syncs end-to-end encrypted through your own iCloud. See the [Privacy Policy](/watchword/privacy/).

### How do I restore my subscription?

If you reinstalled the App or signed in on a new device, tap **Restore Purchases** in Settings. Your subscription will reactivate once Apple confirms it.

### How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID, not through us. To cancel or change your plan:

1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone.
2. Tap your **name** at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Tap **Watchword**, then **Cancel Subscription**.

Detailed instructions: [Apple's subscription help](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202039).

Cancelling stops auto-renewal. You keep access until the end of your current billing period.

### Will I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. Request one at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

### Which iOS versions are supported?

iOS 26.0 and newer. An iCloud account is required.

### Found a bug? Have a feature request?

Email **wearesobackltd@gmail.com** with:

- Your iPhone model and iOS version.
- A short description of what happened.
- A screenshot or screen recording if possible.
